**Title:** `EventListener` (Jitsi Meet SDK events)

**Purpose:** Catalog of events emitted by Jitsi Meet (web SDK / iframe API) with descriptions of the callback object. This file provides a quick reference for event names, the shape of the listener argument, and the meaning of each event.
