The following lists members of **JitsiConnectionErrors** (connection-level errors) in CSV format.

```csv
stt,enum,value,description
1,CONFERENCE_REQUEST_FAILED,"connection.conferenceRequestFailed","Occurs when the conference request to Jicofo failed."
2,CONNECTION_DROPPED_ERROR,"connection.droppedError","Indicates the connection was dropped unexpectedly, typically due to network issues (e.g. BOSH session timeout)."
3,NOT_LIVE_ERROR,"connection.notLiveError","Indicates the conference is not yet live/ready as reported by Jicofo."
4,OTHER_ERROR,"connection.otherError","An unspecified connection error."
5,PASSWORD_REQUIRED,"connection.passwordRequired","Indicates a password is required to join."
6,SERVER_ERROR,"connection.serverError","Indicates the connection was terminated due to repeated server (HTTP 5xx) errors."
7,SHARD_CHANGED_ERROR,"connection.shardChangedError","Indicates the connection was disconnected because the conference was moved to a different shard."
```
